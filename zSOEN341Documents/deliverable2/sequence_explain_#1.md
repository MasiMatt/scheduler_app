 Register for a Course according to a Preference Use Case:
 
   The sequence diagram shown above shows the steps a student takes to 
   
   request to register for a specific course according to his preferences. 
  
    These preferences include choices such as the time, day, and credit preferences
    
    he would like to have. Once these preference selection is completed,
    
    several validation steps are taken in order to ensure that the
    
    student has fulfilled all the necessary requirements to take the course of his choice.
    
    Once this validation is successfully completed, the new entry,in this case
    
    the course he added,  the student's record is updated and the student can 
    
    request for this updated record.
